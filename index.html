Sun
• Type: Yellow dwarf star (G-type main-sequence star)
• Composition: 73% hydrogen, 25% helium, trace elements
• Diameter: ~1.39 million km
• Mass: 1.989 × 10^30 kg (99.86% of the Solar System's mass)
• Surface Temperature: ~5,500°C
• Core Temperature: ~15 million °C
• Energy Source: Nuclear fusion, converting hydrogen to helium
• Light Travel Time to Earth: ~8 minutes
Mercury
• Type: Terrestrial planet
• Diameter: ~4,880 km
• Distance from Sun: ~57.9 million km (0.39 AU)
• Surface Temperature: -173°C to 427°C
• Atmosphere: Exosphere (thin), mainly oxygen, sodium, hydrogen
• Surface Features: Craters, cliffs, and plains
• Rotation Period: 58.6 Earth days
• Revolution Period: 88 Earth days
Venus
• Type: Terrestrial planet
• Diameter: ~12,104 km
• Distance from Sun: ~108.2 million km (0.72 AU)
• Surface Temperature: ~464°C (hottest planet)
• Atmosphere: Thick, mostly carbon dioxide, sulfuric acid clouds
• Surface Features: Volcanoes, mountains, and plains
Earth
• Type: Terrestrial planet
• Diameter: ~12,742 km
• Distance from Sun: ~149.6 million km (1 AU)
• Surface Temperature: -89°C to 58°C (average ~15°C)
• Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, trace gases
• Surface Features: Oceans, mountains, deserts, forests
• Rotation Period: 24 hours
• Revolution Period: 365.25 days
• Natural Satellite: Moon
Mars
• Type: Terrestrial planet
• Diameter: ~6,779 km
• Distance from Sun: ~227.9 million km (1.52 AU)
• Surface Temperature: -125°C to 20°C
• Atmosphere: Thin, mostly carbon dioxide
• Surface Features: Craters, volcanoes, valleys, polar ice caps
• Rotation Period: 24.6 hours
• Revolution Period: 687 Earth days
• Moons: Phobos and Deimos
Jupiter
• Type: Gas giant
• Diameter: ~139,820 km
• Distance from Sun: ~778.5 million km (5.2 AU)
• Surface Temperature: -108°C (cloud tops)
• Atmosphere: Mostly hydrogen and helium • Surface Features: Great Red Spot (storm), cloud bands
• Rotation Period: ~10 hours (fastest rotation)
• Revolution Period: ~12 Earth years
• Moons: 95+ (notable: Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto)

Uranus
• Type: Ice giant
• Diameter: ~50,724 km
• Distance from Sun: ~2.87 billion km (19.2 AU)
• Surface Temperature: -195°C
• Atmosphere: Hydrogen, helium, methane (gives blue color)
• Surface Features: Faint rings, extreme axial tilt (98°)
• Rotation Period: ~17 hours (retrograde)
• Revolution Period: ~84 Earth years
• Moons: 27 (notable: Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania, Oberon)
